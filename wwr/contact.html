Our History

Established in 1995, we have over 25 years of experience crafting unforgettable rafting experiences. Our roots began in the heart of the Rockies, where our passion for nature and adventure combined to form our company.

Today, we are proud to offer world-class rafting tours that showcase the beauty of our rivers while prioritizing safety and sustainability.
