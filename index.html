Poland, Mazowieckie province

Created in 1999 as one of 16 new provinces, the Mazovian Province (Polish: województwo mazowieckie)
is the largest and most populous province in Poland, located in the central-eastern part of the
country. Its capital is Warsaw, which is also Poland's national capital and a major cultural,
political, and economic hub.
